Fried Chicken

Description

Fried chicken is a dish consisting of chicken pieces usually from broiler chickens which have been floured or battered and then pan-fried, deep fried, or pressure fried.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 quart vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the flour, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt and pepper. Mix thoroughly.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg and milk.
  4. Dip each piece of chicken in the egg mixture, and then roll in the flour blend. Repeat so that each piece of chicken is double coated.
  5. Deep fry in the hot oil until the crust is golden brown, and the juices run clear, about 10 minutes.
  6. Drain on paper towels.
